# Vidalyn Folorunso Bioinformatics Portfolio

## About Me
I am a Medical Laboratory Scientific Officer and an Africa CDC Bioinformatics Fellow. I am building skills in genomics and bioinformatics to support infectious disease surveillance and antimicrobial resistance monitoring in Africa.

---

## My Bioinformatics Journey

My training has progressed through:

- Mpox genomics (introduction to sequencing analysis)
- HIV genomics (alignment and variant calling)
- AMR genomics (antimicrobial resistance detection)

---

## Projects

### Mpox Genomics
Analysis of Mpox sequencing data including quality control, trimming, mapping, and variant calling.

### HIV Genomics
Alignment of HIV sequences to reference genome and detection of genomic mutations.

### AMR Genomics
Detection and interpretation of antimicrobial resistance genes in bacterial genomes.
